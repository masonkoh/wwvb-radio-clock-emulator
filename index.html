Signal Logic

Markers are emitted at seconds 0, 9, 19, 29, 39, 49, and 59. Data pulses follow the NIST WWVB AM timing envelope.

Timing Engine

Audio is scheduled with AudioContext.currentTime and gain automation only. No setTimeout or setInterval is used for pulse timing.

Placement Tip

Put the watch as close as possible to the phone or laptop speaker and keep the volume high but clean to maximize the local magnetic field.
